Social Media Checklist — Do's & Don'ts

A quick reference guide for responsible, professional, and safe social media use.


# DO's

Privacy & Security

 Use strong, unique passwords for each platform
 Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
 Review and update your privacy settings regularly
 Log out from shared or public devices
 Check app permissions before granting access

Profile & Identity

 Use your real name and a clear profile photo
 Keep your bio accurate and up to date
 Add your college/institution and field of study
 Link your GitHub / LinkedIn where relevant
 Use a professional email address for sign-up

Content & Posting

 Think before you post — ask "Would I say this in public?"
 Share accurate, verified information only
 Credit original creators when sharing their content
 Use relevant hashtags to increase reach
 Proofread posts for grammar and spelling before publishing
 Post consistently to build a reliable online presence

Interaction & Networking

 Engage respectfully with others' posts
 Respond to comments and messages politely
 Support peers by sharing their achievements
 Join communities relevant to your field (AI, ML, Tech)
 Follow professionals, researchers, and institutions in your domain

Professional Presence

 Share your projects, certificates, and achievements
 Highlight internships and academic milestones
 Connect with professors, mentors, and alumni
 Keep LinkedIn profile updated with skills and experiences
 Use platforms like GitHub to showcase your work


# DON'Ts

Privacy & Security

 Never share your passwords with anyone
 Never share OTPs, PINs, or personal financial details
 Don't accept friend/follow requests from unknown accounts
 Don't click on suspicious or unverified links
 Don't use the same password across multiple platforms

Profile & Identity

 Don't impersonate someone else online
 Don't use fake or misleading profile information
 Don't share your home address or phone number publicly
 Don't leave old/inactive accounts unprotected

Content & Posting

 Don't post in anger or without thinking
 Don't spread unverified news or misinformation
 Don't plagiarize or repost without giving credit
 Don't overshare personal or family information
 Don't post content that could embarrass you professionally
 Don't engage in online arguments or trolling

Interaction & Networking

 Don't cyberbully or harass others
 Don't send unsolicited promotional messages (spam)
 Don't make negative comments about classmates, teachers, or institutions
 Don't share others' private information without consent

Professional Presence

 Don't badmouth employers, colleges, or professors online
 Don't post anything that contradicts your professional image
 Don't ignore privacy settings on LinkedIn and other platforms
 Don't connect with strangers who ask for money or personal favors
